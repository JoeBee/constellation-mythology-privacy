# Privacy Policy for Heavenly Guidance

Last Updated: 5-12-2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Heavenly Guidance. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our app and tell you about your privacy rights.

## Data We Collect

### Personal Data
- **Zodiac Sign**: We store your selected zodiac sign locally on your device to provide personalized horoscope readings.
- **Location Data**: We request access to your device's location to provide accurate constellation information and astronomical data. This data is used in real-time and is not stored permanently.

### Device Data
- **Device Information**: We collect basic device information necessary for the app's functionality, including device orientation for constellation pointing features.
- **App Usage Data**: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience.

## How We Use Your Data

### Location Services
- To provide accurate constellation information based on your current location
- To calculate astronomical positions and horoscope data
- Location data is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently

### Text-to-Speech
- To provide audio narration of horoscope readings
- To enhance accessibility features
- Audio processing is done locally on your device

### User Preferences
- To remember your zodiac sign selection
- To personalize your experience
- Stored locally on your device

### Google AI Integration
- To generate personalized horoscope readings
- Your zodiac sign and current date are used to create relevant content
- Data is processed securely through Google's AI services

## Data Storage and Security

- Most data is stored locally on your device
- We use secure methods to transmit data when necessary
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to processing of your data
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:
- Google AI Services (for horoscope generation)
- Google Play Services (for app distribution)
- These services have their own privacy policies

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
